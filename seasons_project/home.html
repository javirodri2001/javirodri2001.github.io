Seasons in Helena, MT

The weather in Helena is pretty crazy, and so are their seasons. In fact, something important that you should know is that, regardless of the time of the year you visit or just happen to be here, always pack as if it was about to snow. Do not trust the sun you may see, it probably is about to snow. Have always both sunscreen and a scarf, gloves and a thick hat within reach. Better safe than sorry around here.
