Innova-T

Acting consequently in the present with an eye on the future

At newly formed Innova-T, based in San Sebastian de los Reyes, Spain, we invest our time and resources on innovative ideas with the potential of becoming game changers that will help all of us have a better, simpler life in the future. Whether it is by sharing information and making some problems be heard by the society, by putting pressure on public or private institutions to make them act consequently to solve the problems we have to or will have to face in the future, or by making our own solution plans ourselves based on public information within everyone's reach, we go above and beyond as a non-profit to make life easier and better for you.

Be welcome to fight alongside us for the best future possible for the people
